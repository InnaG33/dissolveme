The model uses Charles M. Hansen Solubility Data with Neural Network algorithm to estimate solubility, when trained on types and numbers of chemical elements in its unit molecular formula with the total polymer molecular weight. Ease of use can compensate on the current predicting accuarcy. The studied polymers and resins are shown here.

The solubility was divided into three major categories: soluble (1 = soluble, 2 = almost soluble), 'theta' condition for a neutral interaction (3 = strongly swollen, 4 = swollen), and non-soluble (5 = slight effect, 6 = no visible effect).
